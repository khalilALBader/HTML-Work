LINEAR EQUATIONS

An Equations that has maximum degree one is as Linear Equations . The graph of it is straight line.

For Example :

  • y = 8x-2
  • ax1 + bx2 - cx3 = 9

y1 = mx1a and

y2 = mx2b

m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)
